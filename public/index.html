About Nova Artists

Nova Artist is an actor’s agency based in Northern Ireland. At Nova we believe in finding unique and original talent for all areas of the creative industry and endeavour to provide our clients with the opportunity to work consistently in Ireland and the UKs burgeoning performance sector.

We represent a broad spectrum of professional actors from the newly graduated to established professionals, and offer unparalleled support to all of the talent on our books. We see ourselves as a client centred agency with a personal touch.

Nova Artists is run by Michael and Colin, they have over 30 years of combined experience in the industry and hope to have many more. If you would like to contact us regarding any of our clients, or have any questions please don’t hesitate to contact either of us at michael@novaartists.co.uk or colin@novaartists.co.uk
